About Me

I’m MD Shameer, a student optometrist from India pursuing my Bachelor's degree at the Elite School of Optometry, SASTRA University. I’m passionate about vision science, visual search, and community eye care. Outside clinics, I enjoy anime, music, and learning tech tools!

My Journey

My clinical experience includes internships at leading eye hospitals like Sankara Nethralaya (Chennai), Lotus Eye Hospital (Coimbatore), and the National Institute for Empowerment of Persons with Multiple Disabilities. I've participated in occupational optometry camps and school screenings to improve eye care accessibility.

My current research focuses on visual search efficiency, perception, and real-world task performance.
